# 8. Closing Statement

**Part:** 1 — Philosophy  
**Status:** Draft  
**Type:** Informative

IdeaMark is an intellectual activity infrastructure.

It does not preserve how people think.

It preserves reusable structures that enable future humans and AI to begin thinking from authoritative original sources under new situations.

By reducing the cost of accessing intellectual activities rather than prescribing interpretations, IdeaMark aims to promote richer collaboration across disciplines, organizations, cultures, and generations.

The long-term vision of IdeaMark is an AI-enabled society in which human thinking is amplified through dynamic interpretation, shared access to original sources, and reusable intellectual activity structures.
